There are many different kinds of cookies, but my personal favorite is chocolate chip. Cookies originated in Persia (now Iran) and overtime evolved to be a sweet treat for people of all classes to enjoy. They are most commonly eaten with milk. When it comes to these cookies, I prefer them to be soft and have chunks of chocolate (there are chocolate chunk cookies but I consider them the same as chocolate chip).
