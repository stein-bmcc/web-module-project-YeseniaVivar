Pozole is a Mexican dish, specifically a soup. It is made with hominy, meat (usually chicken or pork), and other seasonings aded into the broth. This soup is also often served with toppings such as shredded lettuce, onions, radishes, and lime. Salsa can also be added to it to make pozole rojo (red pozole) or pozole verde (green pozole). I really like pozole for it's taste and the contrast of the soft meat and the crunchy vegetables.
